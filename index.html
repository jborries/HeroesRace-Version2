About Us

Our Mission

The Heroes Race was started to raise money in support of Heroes and provide funds for Heroes to continue their work. J Anthony Group recognizes that there are different types of Heroes, from Teachers, Soldiers, and Police Officers to many other Role Models that make an impact. The Heroes Race would like to support two organizations for their outstanding dedication to their cause, Big Brothers Big Sisters and Boot Campaign.

TO DONATE BY CHECK:
Please make check out to “Heroes Race” and mail to 1701 River Run, Suite 1015, Fort Worth, Texas 76017.

TO DONATE ONLINE:
Make donations online, securely, using PayPal. *Please note that when donating by credit card there is a 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction fee. PayPal - The safer, easier way to pay online!

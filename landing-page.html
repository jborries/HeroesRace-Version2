Project Worthy

Recognizing and rewarding our local everyday community heroes.

When people think of Heroes, most mention our military, police officers, and firefighters. Often overlooked are the local community doctors, nurses, teachers, mentors, those that are doing an outstanding job giving back to their community, or those that have performed a heroic act that made a difference in the life of someone else. We believe heroes come in all shapes, sizes, and from all walks of life. It is our mission to find these worthy individuals and recognize them in the community.

Join us for our next event as we recognize and reward our local everyday community heroes at The Fort Worth Heroes Race.

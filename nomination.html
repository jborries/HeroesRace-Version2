Nominate A Hero

When people think of Heroes, most mention our military, police officers, and fire firefighters, but many times people don't think of the doctors, nurses, teachers, mentors, those that are doing an outstanding job giving back to their community, or someone performing a heroic one-time act that made an impactful difference in someone's life. Heroes come in all shapes and sizes. We are asking for your help to find local heroes that are worthy of being recognized. Who is your hero?

25

Help us find 25 individuals worthy of being called a hero to the community. These individuals will be showcased along with their story on our website and receive a small monetary gift as a thank you. of these 25 individuals, one will be selected by our committee and honored at our annual "Heroes Race" event. This Hero will receive a $1000 gift as a thank you for their actions.

In order to be showcased, there are set criteria that must be met:

The person being nominated must currently reside or work within Tarrant County.

Heroes cannot nominate themselves.

Do they fit one of the following examples:

  • Inspiration to others: Does your hero serve as an example and inspire others?
  • Public Service: Has you Hero gone above and beyond the call of duty?
  • Outstanding Citizen: Is your Hero unselfish and/or have they displayed a concern for others welfare?
  • Heroic Act: Has your Hero performed an act that took great courage?
  • Overcome hardships and lead by example: Has your Hero faced challenges or obstacles and overcome them?
  • Philanthropic Involvement: Is your Hero making an impact on the community through philanthropy or volunteering?
